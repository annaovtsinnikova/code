The lord of the rings

I genuinely admire this film series. It consists of 3 movies and is based on J. R. R. Tolkien books.
The story is set in fictional world of Middle Earth and tells about the hobbit Frodo Baggins as he
and the other characters try to destroy the One Ring and save the world from the evil Lord Sauron.
